Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

SUM-IT-UP is designed with a "Privacy First" architecture. We believe your reading habits and personal data should remain yours. Unlike cloud-based extensions, SUM-IT-UP processes data locally on your machine.

Data Collection

We collect nothing.

  • No analytics tracking
  • No user accounts or logins
  • No remote data logging

Data Processing

All summarization happens through a local Flask server running on your device (localhost). When you summarize a page:

  • The content is sent from your browser to your local server (127.0.0.1).
  • Processing (cleaning, prompt building) happens locally.
  • The final prompt is sent to the Google Gemini API (if configured) solely for generation.
  • No content is stored on our servers because we don't have any servers.

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API: If you use the Gemini model, text snippets are sent to Google for processing. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy regarding their API data usage.

YouTube: When summarizing videos, the extension accesses the YouTube transcript or audio stream directly from your browser to your local server.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, open an issue on our GitHub repository.
